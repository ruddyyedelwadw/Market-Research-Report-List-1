<p><p>The technical jacket market can be divided into four main types: hardshell jackets, insulated jackets, puffy jackets, and softshell jackets. Hardshell jackets are waterproof and provide protection against harsh weather conditions. Insulated jackets are designed to keep the wearer warm by trapping heat. Puffy jackets are lightweight and provide insulation through layers of down or synthetic materials. Softshell jackets are breathable and flexible, making them a versatile option for outdoor activities. Each type caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the technical jacket market.</p></p>
