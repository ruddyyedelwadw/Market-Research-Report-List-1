<p><p>Superabsorbing Polymers (SAP) are a type of water-absorbent polymer that can hold a large amount of liquid relative to their own mass. There are two main types of SAP markets: Low-Density Cross-Linked SAP and High-Density Cross-Linked SAP. Low-Density Cross-Linked SAP has a lower density and can absorb large amounts of fluid quickly, making it ideal for use in hygiene products. On the other hand, High-Density Cross-Linked SAP has a higher density and is used in applications where gel strength and absorption under pressure are crucial.</p></p>
