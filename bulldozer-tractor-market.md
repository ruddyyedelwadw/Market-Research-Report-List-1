<p><p>Bulldozer tractors are categorized into two main types based on their propulsion system: track type and wheel type. Track-type bulldozers are equipped with continuous tracks for better maneuverability in rough terrains, making them ideal for heavy-duty construction and mining applications. On the other hand, wheel-type bulldozers have conventional wheels that provide faster speeds and better fuel efficiency, making them suitable for lighter construction tasks and road-building projects. Both types cater to different market demands and are designed to meet specific job requirements.</p></p>
