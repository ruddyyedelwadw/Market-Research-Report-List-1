<p><p>Behcet's disease is a chronic, autoimmune inflammatory condition that affects various parts of the body, including the mouth, genitals, eyes, skin, and gastrointestinal system. Additionally, pain relievers, such as nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), can help manage pain and inflammation.</p><p>Lifestyle modifications also play a crucial role in managing Behcet's disease.
