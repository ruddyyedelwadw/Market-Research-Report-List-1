<p><p>Medical cylinder valves come in two main types: right-hand threaded valve outlets and left-hand threaded valve outlets. Right-hand threaded valve outlets are the most common type and are used for gases like oxygen and helium. Left-hand threaded valve outlets are primarily used for gases like nitrogen and carbon dioxide. Each type of valve outlet is designed to ensure compatibility and safety when connecting gas cylinders to medical equipment, making it essential for healthcare providers to choose the correct type for their specific needs.</p></p>
