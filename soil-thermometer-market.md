<p><p>Soil thermometers are categorized into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I soil thermometers are designed for general use and provide accurate temperature readings in various soil types and conditions. They are commonly used in agriculture, gardening, and landscaping. Type II soil thermometers are specifically designed for scientific research and precision temperature measurement in specialized fields such as soil science, environmental studies, and geology. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring and managing soil temperature for optimal plant growth and environmental conditions.</p></p>
