<p><p>Paint rollers are categorized into various types based on the material of the roller cover. Woven paint rollers are made of tightly woven fabric, providing a smooth and even finish. Knit paint rollers, on the other hand, are made of knitted fabric, suitable for rough surfaces and textured coatings. Other types of paint rollers include foam rollers, which are ideal for smooth surfaces, and microfiber rollers, which provide excellent absorption and release of paint. Each type has unique characteristics to cater to different painting needs.</p></p>
