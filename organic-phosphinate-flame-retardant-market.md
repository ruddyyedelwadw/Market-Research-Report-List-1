<p><p>Organic phosphinate flame retardants are classified into two main types: phosphate and phosphite market. Phosphate flame retardants are primarily derived from phosphoric acid and are known for their high thermal stability and effectiveness in reducing flame spread. On the other hand, phosphite flame retardants are synthesized from phosphorous acid and are favored for their compatibility with various polymers and ability to provide excellent thermal stability. Both phosphate and phosphite flame retardants play a crucial role in enhancing the fire safety of numerous products and materials.</p></p>
