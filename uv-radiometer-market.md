<p><p>UV Radiometers can be categorized into two main types based on their purpose: specific purpose UV Radiometers and multifunctional UV Radiometers. Specific purpose UV Radiometers are designed for measuring specific parameters such as UV-A, UV-B, or UV-C radiation levels, while multifunctional UV Radiometers are capable of measuring multiple parameters simultaneously. Specific purpose UV Radiometers are ideal for applications where precise measurements of a single parameter are required, while multifunctional UV Radiometers are more versatile and suitable for a wide range of applications that require monitoring of multiple parameters.</p></p>
