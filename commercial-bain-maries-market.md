<p><strong>The Commercial Bain Maries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Heat Bain Maries</li><li>Dry Heat Bain Maries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Bain Maries come in two main types: Wet Heat and Dry Heat. Wet Heat Bain Maries use steam to heat and maintain the temperature of the food, making them ideal for dishes that need to be kept moist. On the other hand, Dry Heat Bain Maries use radiant heat or convection heating elements to keep food warm without adding moisture, making them perfect for items like fried foods or baked goods. Both types are popular choices in commercial kitchens for keeping food at a consistent temperature during service.</p></p>
