<p><strong>The Automatic Pulp Moulding Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Pulp Moulding Machines</li><li>Reciprocating Pulp Moulding Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Pulp Moulding Machines are classified into two main types: Rotary Pulp Moulding Machines and Reciprocating Pulp Moulding Machines. Rotary machines have a rotating molds system that produces products continuously, while reciprocating machines have a back-and-forth movement in the forming process. Rotary machines are known for their high production capacity and efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. Reciprocating machines, on the other hand, are more suitable for smaller production runs and offer flexibility in design changes.</p></p>
