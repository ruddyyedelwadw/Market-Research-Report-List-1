<p><strong>The Screw Capping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semi-automatic screw capping systems are operated partially by hand and partially by a machine. Operators manually place the cap on the container, then the machine tightens the cap. These systems are often used for small-scale production or when flexibility is required in cap sizes. Fully automatic screw capping systems are completely machine-operated, with caps being placed and tightened automatically. These systems are ideal for high-volume production and offer faster and more efficient capping processes.</p></p>
