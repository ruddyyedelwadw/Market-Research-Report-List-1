<p><p>Truck Volume Measurement Systems can be categorized into two main types: Static Measurement Systems and Dynamic Measurement Systems. Static Measurement Systems involve stationary equipment that measures the volume of a truck's cargo while it is at rest. On the other hand, Dynamic Measurement Systems utilize technology that allows for volume measurement while the truck is in motion. Each type offers unique benefits and applications depending on the specific needs of the user, such as accuracy, efficiency, and real-time data collection.</p></p>
