<p><p>The cold chain warehousing market includes various types of facilities designed to store and transport perishable goods at low temperatures to maintain their quality. Blast freezers and chillers are larger facilities that rapidly freeze or cool products to preserve their freshness. Cold rooms are specially insulated rooms with controlled temperatures used for long-term storage. These different types of facilities are crucial for the supply chain of industries like food and pharmaceuticals that rely on keeping products chilled or frozen.</p></p>
