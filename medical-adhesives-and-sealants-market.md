<p><p>Medical adhesives and sealants market is segmented into three main types: acrylic, silicone, and rubber. Silicone adhesives are biocompatible and flexible, often used for skin contact applications. Rubber sealants provide an excellent barrier against moisture and chemicals, commonly used for medical device bonding. Each type offers unique properties to cater to different medical needs, ensuring safe and effective patient care.</p></p>
