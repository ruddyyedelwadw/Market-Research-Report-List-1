FOS and GOS are naturally occurring prebiotics found in certain foods, while Synanthrin is a synthetic prebiotic. Polydextrose is a soluble fiber prebiotic, and IMO is derived from starch. Resistant Dextrin is a resistant starch prebiotic. These prebiotics promote the growth of beneficial bacteria in the gut, improving digestive health and overall well-being.</p></p>
