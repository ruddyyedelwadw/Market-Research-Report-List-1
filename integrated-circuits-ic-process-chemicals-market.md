Dry film is used for imaging high-quality circuit patterns, while inks are used for printing conductive and resistive materials. ENIG is a surface finish used to protect the copper traces on PCBs. Photoresist is a light-sensitive material used for photolithography. Etchant is used to remove unwanted material during the circuit fabrication process. Contrast medium enhances image quality in IC production, and other markets include various chemicals used in different stages of the IC fabrication process.</p></p>
