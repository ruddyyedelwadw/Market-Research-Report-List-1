<p><ul><li>Single layer</li><li>Multilayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HDF laminate flooring is available in two main market types: single layer and multilayer. Single layer flooring is composed of a single layer of HDF (high-density fiberboard) with the decorative print and protective wear layer applied on top. Multilayer flooring, on the other hand, has additional layers such as an underlayment for sound reduction and moisture protection. Single layer flooring tends to be more cost-effective and easier to install, while multilayer flooring offers enhanced durability and performance benefits.</p></p>
