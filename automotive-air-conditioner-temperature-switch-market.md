<p><ul><li>Bimetallic Strip Switch</li><li>Liquid Filled Temperature Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Air Conditioner Temperature Switch market includes two main types: Bimetallic Strip Switch and Liquid Filled Temperature Switch. Bimetallic Strip Switches work by using two different metals with different thermal expansion coefficients to control the switch operation based on temperature changes. On the other hand, Liquid Filled Temperature Switches use a liquid-filled bulb attached to a temperature-measuring device to regulate the switch operation. Both types are commonly used in automotive air conditioning systems to help maintain a comfortable interior temperature.</p></p>
