<p><p>Oxyfuel cutting machines are available in three main types: CNC (Computer Numerical Control), Manually-controlled, and PLC-controlled. CNC machines are operated by a computer program, allowing for precise and automated cutting. Manually-controlled machines are operated by hand, offering more flexibility but requiring more skill. PLC-controlled machines are operated using programmable logic controllers, providing a balance between automation and manual control. Each type of machine caters to different levels of precision, skill requirements, and production needs in the oxyfuel cutting market.</p></p>
