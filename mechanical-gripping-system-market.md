<p><ul><li>Parallel Gripping System</li><li>Centric Gripping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mechanical Gripping System market includes two main types - Parallel Gripping System and Centric Gripping System. Parallel Gripping System involves jaws that move parallel to each other to grip the object, providing a secure hold. On the other hand, Centric Gripping System involves jaws that move towards the center of the object to grip it, ensuring precise positioning. Both systems are commonly used in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and electronics for tasks like picking, placing, and transferring objects with efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
