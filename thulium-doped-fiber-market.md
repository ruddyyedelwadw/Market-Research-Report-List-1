<p><p>Thulium-doped fiber is used in two main types of markets: single clad fiber and double clad fiber. Single clad fiber consists of a single core where the thulium ions are doped, allowing for the amplification of signals in optical communication systems. On the other hand, double clad fiber has an additional inner cladding layer that allows for higher power handling capabilities, making it ideal for applications that require higher amplification levels. Both types of fibers play a crucial role in the telecommunications and medical industries.</p></p>
