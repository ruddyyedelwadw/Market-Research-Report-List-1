<p><p>Heat sinks and cooling fins are devices used in electronics to dissipate heat and maintain optimal operating temperatures. Heat sinks are typically made from materials with high thermal conductivity, such as aluminum or copper, and are attached to electronic components to absorb and transfer heat away. Cooling fins, on the other hand, are thin protrusions that increase the surface area for heat dissipation.</p><p>The Heat Sinks and Cooling Fins Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.80% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for electronics in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, telecommunications, and consumer electronics. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on enhancing the design and materials used in heat sinks and cooling fins to make them more efficient and lightweight.</p></p>
