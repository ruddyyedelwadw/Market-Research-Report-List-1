<p><p>Monitor headphones (In-ear monitors) market consists of two main types: Dynamic headphones and Moving iron headphones. Dynamic headphones use a diaphragm driven by a coil and magnet to produce sound, offering a rich and dynamic audio experience. Moving iron headphones, on the other hand, use a miniature driver unit that provides high-resolution sound and detailed audio reproduction. Both types cater to professional musicians, audio engineers, and audiophiles seeking high-quality sound monitoring for performances, recording, and listening experiences.</p></p>
