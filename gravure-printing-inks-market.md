<p><p>Gravure printing inks are used in the printing industry for high-quality, long-run printing on various substrates. There are two main types of gravure printing inks on the market: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based inks are known for their fast drying time and excellent print quality, making them suitable for packaging and publications. On the other hand, water-based inks are environmentally friendly and offer low VOC emissions, making them ideal for food packaging and other sensitive applications. Each type of ink offers unique benefits based on specific printing requirements.</p></p>
