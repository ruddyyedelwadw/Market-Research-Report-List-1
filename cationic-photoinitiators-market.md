<p><p>Cationic photoinitiators are divided into three main types: onium salt photoinitiators, metal-organic photoinitiators, and organosilane photoinitiators. Onium salt photoinitiators are widely used in UV-curable systems due to their high reactivity and efficiency. Metal-organic photoinitiators are known for their excellent performance in initiating polymerization reactions. Organosilane photoinitiators are preferred for their good compatibility with various resin systems. Each type of cationic photoinitiator offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
