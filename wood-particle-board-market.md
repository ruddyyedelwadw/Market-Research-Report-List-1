<p><p>Wood particle board is commonly categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I particle board is typically used in applications where moisture resistance is a priority, such as in humid environments or in outdoor furniture. On the other hand, Type II particle board is more commonly used in general indoor applications due to its lower moisture resistance. Both types of particle board offer cost-effective and versatile options for various construction and furniture projects.</p></p>
