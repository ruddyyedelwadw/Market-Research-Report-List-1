<p><p>NDIR CO2 sensors, or Non-Dispersive Infrared sensors, are devices used to measure carbon dioxide levels in a given environment. These sensors work by emitting infrared light through the sample gas and measuring the absorption of light to determine the concentration of CO2 present. NDIR CO2 sensors are widely used in HVAC systems, indoor air quality monitoring, industrial process control, and automotive applications.</p><p>The NDIR CO2 sensor market is experiencing steady growth, with a projected CAGR of 3.10% during the forecast period. The increasing focus on indoor air quality and the growing awareness of the impact of CO2 emissions on climate change are driving the demand for NDIR CO2 sensors. Additionally, stringent government regulations regarding emissions control and the need for energy-efficient solutions are further propelling market growth.</p><p>Key trends in the NDIR CO2 sensor market include advancements in sensor technology, such as miniaturization and improved accuracy. There is also a growing emphasis on wireless connectivity and IoT integration, allowing for remote monitoring and control of CO2 levels. The market is seeing a rise in demand for NDIR CO2 sensors in various industries, including agriculture, healthcare, and transportation, as companies strive to reduce their carbon footprint and improve overall environmental sustainability.</p></p>
