<p><p>Water-washed Kaolin market can be categorized into two types based on the method of separation: Strong Magnetic Separation and General Magnetic Separation. Strong Magnetic Separation involves using highly powerful magnets to extract kaolin particles from water, resulting in a high-purity product. Both methods are widely used in the industry to produce different grades of water-washed kaolin for various applications.</p></p>
