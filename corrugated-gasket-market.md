<p><p>Corrugated gaskets are commonly made from two types of materials - semi-metallic and metallic. Semi-metallic gaskets are made from a combination of synthetic and metallic materials, providing a strong seal for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. On the other hand, metallic gaskets are made entirely from metal, such as stainless steel or carbon steel, offering excellent durability and resistance to extreme conditions. Both types of gaskets are widely used in industries such as oil & gas, chemical processing, and power generation for sealing applications.</p></p>
