<p><ul><li>Regular Screws</li><li>Cranking Screws</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ice screws are essential tools for ice climbing and mountaineering, available in two main types - Regular Screws and Cranking Screws. Regular screws feature a sharper pointed tip and are easier to place in solid ice. They are ideal for quick and easy placements. On the other hand, Cranking screws have a larger thread pitch and a more aggressive tooth design, providing better grip in softer ice. They are designed for placements in more difficult ice conditions, providing a secure anchor for climbers.</p></p>
