<p><strong>The Rigid Insulation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Expanded Polystyrene (EPS)</li><li>Extruded Polystyrene (XPS)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rigid insulation materials are primarily categorized into Expanded Polystyrene (EPS), Extruded Polystyrene (XPS), and others. EPS is known for its excellent insulation properties and cost-effectiveness, making it a popular choice for construction projects. XPS offers higher compressive strength and moisture resistance compared to EPS, making it suitable for areas with high moisture levels. Other types of rigid insulation include polyisocyanurate and phenolic foam, which offer different benefits such as higher R-values and fire resistance.</p></p>
