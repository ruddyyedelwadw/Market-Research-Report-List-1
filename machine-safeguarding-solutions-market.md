Sensors detect hazards such as light curtains that ensure operator safety by interrupting a machine's operation if an object is detected. Other solutions may include safety mats or interlock devices that prevent access to hazardous areas. These market types collectively provide a range of options for safeguarding machines and protecting workers from potential accidents or injuries.</p></p>
