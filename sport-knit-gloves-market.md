<p><p>Sport knit gloves come in two main market types: half-finger and full-finger gloves. Half-finger gloves, also known as fingerless gloves, provide coverage up to the first knuckle of the fingers, leaving the fingertips exposed. These gloves offer better dexterity and grip, making them suitable for activities requiring precise finger movements. On the other hand, full-finger gloves cover the entire finger, offering maximum protection and warmth. These gloves are commonly used in cold environments or for sports activities where finger insulation and enhanced hand coverage are required.</p></p>
