<p><p>Air stripper systems are used to remove volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and other contaminants from water or wastewater. Two common types of air stripper systems are the Sieve Tray System and Packed Tower System. The Sieve Tray System uses trays with holes to allow air and water to come into contact, while the Packed Tower System uses packing material to increase surface area for contact. Both systems work by stripping contaminants from the water and transferring them to the air.</p></p>
