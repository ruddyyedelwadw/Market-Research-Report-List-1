<p><p>Power discrete modules are electronic components used in power electronics systems for controlling and regulating electrical power. There are different types of power discrete modules available in the market including standard (non-integrated) IGBT modules, intelligent power modules, thyristor/diode modules (and rectifier bridges), power integrated modules, and MOSFET modules. Standard IGBT modules are basic power switches, intelligent power modules offer advanced functionality, thyristor/diode modules are used for high power applications, power integrated modules combine multiple functions, and MOSFET modules are used for high efficiency power control.</p></p>
