<p><p>Automotive automatic seat belts come in different types: Two-point type, Three-point type, and Above Four-point type. The Two-point type secures the lap and shoulder with two attachment points. The Three-point type adds an additional attachment point over the shoulder for improved safety. The Above Four-point type further enhances safety with additional attachment points across the chest and abdomen. Each type offers varying levels of protection and comfort, catering to different preferences and safety requirements in the automotive market.</p></p>
