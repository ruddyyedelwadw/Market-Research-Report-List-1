<p><strong>The Contactless Readers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Contactless Readers</li><li>Mobile Contactless Readers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fixed Contactless Readers are stationary devices typically mounted in a fixed location such as a point of sale terminal. They are commonly used in retail, transportation, and access control applications. Mobile Contactless Readers, on the other hand, are portable devices that can be carried around by users for on-the-go transactions. They are often used in situations where fixed readers are not practical, such as in events or for mobile ticketing. Both types of readers use radio frequency identification (RFID) or Near Field Communication (NFC) technology to securely transmit data for contactless transactions.</p></p>
