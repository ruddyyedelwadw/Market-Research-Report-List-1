<p><ul><li>Dedicated Injection</li><li>Annular Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cuttings Re-injection Services involve injecting drilling waste back into the ground to prevent environmental pollution. Dedicated Injection involves drilling a separate well solely for cuttings disposal, ensuring proper containment and isolation of waste. Annular Injection involves injecting cuttings into the annulus of an existing well, typically practiced in areas with limited space or where dedicated wells are not feasible. Both methods help in minimizing environmental impact and promoting sustainable drilling practices.</p></p>
