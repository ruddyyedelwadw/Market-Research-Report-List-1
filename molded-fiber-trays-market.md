<p><p>Molded fiber trays are commonly classified into two market types: recycled paper and pulp, and primary pulp. Recycled paper and pulp trays are made from post-consumer waste materials, offering a sustainable packaging solution. On the other hand, primary pulp trays are made from virgin fibers obtained directly from trees. Both types of trays are commonly used in packaging applications due to their eco-friendly nature and versatility. The choice between the two types often depends on factors such as cost, availability, and sustainability goals.</p></p>
