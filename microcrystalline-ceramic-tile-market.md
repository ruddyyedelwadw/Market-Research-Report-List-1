<p><p>The Microcrystalline Ceramic Tile Market includes three main types: Whole Body Microcrystalline, Microcrystalline Composite Board, and Non-porous Microcrystalline. Whole Body Microcrystalline tiles are made from the same material throughout the tile, ensuring that any wear and tear will be less noticeable. Microcrystalline Composite Board tiles are constructed with multiple layers, offering added strength and durability. Non-porous Microcrystalline tiles have a low water absorption rate, making them ideal for areas that are prone to moisture. Each type caters to different needs in the market.</p></p>
