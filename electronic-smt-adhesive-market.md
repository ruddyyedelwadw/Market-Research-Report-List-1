<p><ul><li>One Component</li><li>Multicomponent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic SMT adhesive market is segmented into two types: one-component and multicomponent adhesives. One-component adhesives are pre-mixed and do not require any additional mixing before application, making them easy to use. Multicomponent adhesives, on the other hand, require mixing of multiple components before application, offering better performance and durability compared to one-component adhesives. Both types cater to different needs in the electronics industry, with one-component adhesives being preferred for simplicity and convenience, while multicomponent adhesives are chosen for specialized applications requiring high performance.</p></p>
