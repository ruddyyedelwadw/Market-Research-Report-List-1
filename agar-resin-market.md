Powder agar resin is finely ground and easy to dissolve, making it suitable for applications where a smooth consistency is required. Granules are larger particles that can provide a slower release of agar properties and are commonly used in controlled-release applications. Strips are thin sheets of agar resin that can be easily cut and shaped for specific purposes. Each type has unique characteristics that cater to different needs in various industries.</p></p>
