<p><ul><li>Induction Tachogenerators</li><li>Synchronous Tachogenerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC Tachometer Generators are devices used to measure speed in various industrial applications. There are two main types of AC Tachometer Generators: Induction Tachogenerators and Synchronous Tachogenerators. Induction Tachogenerators use electromagnetic induction to create a voltage proportional to the speed of the rotating shaft. Synchronous Tachogenerators use a permanent magnet to generate a voltage that is synchronized with the speed of the shaft. Both types are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing for speed control and monitoring.</p></p>
