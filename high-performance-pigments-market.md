<p><p>High Performance Pigments (HPP) can be classified into two main types: Department of Organic HPP and Department of Inorganic HPP. </p><p>Department of Organic HPP includes pigments that are carbon-based and synthesized from various organic compounds. These pigments offer high color strength, durability, and chemical resistance.</p><p>Department of Inorganic HPP consists of pigments that are mineral-based and derived from inorganic compounds. These pigments provide excellent heat resistance, lightfastness, and stability.</p><p>Both types of HPP are widely used in various industries for their superior performance characteristics.</p></p>
