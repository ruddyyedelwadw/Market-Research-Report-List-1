RTC is a type of clock that provides accurate timekeeping, commonly used in electronic devices requiring precise time. Semiconductor Clock IC, on the other hand, is an integrated circuit that integrates clock functions into a single chip, offering higher performance and reducing the need for external components. Both types cater to different market needs and contribute to the overall growth and advancement of clock technology.</p></p>
