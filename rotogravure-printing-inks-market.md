<p><p>Rotogravure Printing Inks market consists of four main types based on the binder used in the ink formulation: Nitrocellulose, Polyamide, Polyurethane, and Acrylic inks. Nitrocellulose inks are known for their fast drying time, Polyamide inks offer excellent adhesion and abrasion resistance, Polyurethane inks provide high flexibility and chemical resistance, while Acrylic inks are known for their high gloss and color retention properties. Each type of ink caters to specific printing requirements and substrate types in the rotogravure printing industry.</p></p>
