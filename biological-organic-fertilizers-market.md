<p><p>Biological organic fertilizers can be classified into two main types: microorganism-based fertilizers and organic residue-based fertilizers. Microorganism-based fertilizers contain beneficial bacteria or fungi that help improve soil health and nutrient absorption by plants. Organic residue-based fertilizers are derived from natural substances like compost, manure, or plant residues. Both types of fertilizers are considered environmentally friendly and sustainable alternatives to chemical fertilizers, promoting soil health and enhancing crop productivity.</p></p>
