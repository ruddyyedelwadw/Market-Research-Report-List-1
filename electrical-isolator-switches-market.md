<p><strong>The Electrical Isolator Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrical isolator switches are used to disconnect electrical circuit when needed for maintenance or repair. Single break isolators have one set of contacts to break the circuit, while double break isolators have two sets of contacts for added safety. Pantograph type isolators use a parallelogram mechanism to move the contacts in a vertical motion. Each type of isolator switch offers different advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the electrical system.</p></p>
