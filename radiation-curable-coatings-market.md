<p><strong>The Radiation Curable Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV-Curable</li><li>Electron Beam Curable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiation curable coatings include UV-curable and Electron Beam curable coatings. UV-curable coatings use ultraviolet light to initiate the curing process, creating a hard, durable finish on various surfaces. Electron Beam curable coatings, on the other hand, utilize high-energy electrons to initiate the curing process, resulting in a fast and efficient curing method. Both types of coatings offer benefits such as fast curing times, reduced energy consumption, and low volatile organic compound emissions, making them popular choices in industries such as automotive, packaging, and electronics.</p></p>
