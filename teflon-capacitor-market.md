<p><strong>The Teflon Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Teflon Capacitor</li><li>Radial Teflon Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Teflon capacitors are classified into two main types: Axial Teflon Capacitors and Radial Teflon Capacitors. Axial Teflon Capacitors have leads extending from both ends, making them suitable for through-hole mounting applications. On the other hand, Radial Teflon Capacitors have leads that come off from one side, making them more ideal for surface mounting applications. Both types of Teflon capacitors offer high precision, stability, and reliability, making them popular choices for a wide range of electronic applications.</p></p>
