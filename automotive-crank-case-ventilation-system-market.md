<p><p>The Automotive Crank Case Ventilation System market includes two main types: Natural Ventilation System and Forced Ventilation System. </p><p>Natural Ventilation System relies on the natural pressure differences within the engine to expel gases from the crankcase. On the other hand, Forced Ventilation System uses a dedicated mechanism, such as a ventilation fan, to actively remove gases from the crankcase. </p><p>Each system has its own advantages and is used based on the specific requirements and performance goals of the vehicle.</p></p>
