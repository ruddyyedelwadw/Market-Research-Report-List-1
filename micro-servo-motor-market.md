<p><p>Micro servo motors are available in two main types: DC and AC. DC servo motors are powered by direct current and are known for their precise control and high efficiency. They are widely used in applications that require accuracy and reliability, such as robotics and automation. On the other hand, AC servo motors are powered by alternating current and are ideal for high-speed and high-torque applications. They are commonly used in industrial machinery and manufacturing equipment. Both types of servo motors offer unique advantages and are tailored to different requirements in the market.</p></p>
