<p><p>Multilayer coupled inductors are available in two main market types based on their core material: low frequency core and high frequency core. Low frequency core inductors are designed for applications with lower operating frequencies, offering high inductance values and low losses. On the other hand, high frequency core inductors are optimized for higher frequencies, providing efficient energy storage and minimal signal distortion. Both types cater to specific needs of different electronic systems and play a crucial role in power conversion and signal processing applications.</p></p>
