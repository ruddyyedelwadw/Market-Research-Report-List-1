<p><p>The dental laboratory micro-sealers market comprises three types: laser, infrared, and pulse ARC. Laser micro-sealers use focused laser beams to create a seal on dental material surfaces, ensuring durability. Infrared micro-sealers utilize heat radiation to generate a robust, long-lasting bond. Pulse ARC micro-sealers rely on a controlled electrical discharge to form a strong seal, providing excellent adhesion. These micro-sealers play a crucial role in dental laboratories, ensuring the effectiveness and reliability of dental materials and their applications.</p></p>
