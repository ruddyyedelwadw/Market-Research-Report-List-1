<p><p>Wireless guiding systems come in two main market types: Digital Signal Transmission and Analog Signal Transmission. Digital Signal Transmission refers to systems that convert sound or data into a digital format before transmitting it wirelessly. This method offers higher quality and more reliable transmission compared to analog systems. On the other hand, Analog Signal Transmission involves sending sound or data wirelessly without converting it into a digital format. While analog systems may be simpler and more cost-effective, they typically have lower quality and reliability compared to digital systems.</p></p>
