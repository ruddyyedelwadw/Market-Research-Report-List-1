<p><p>Cotton fibre and yarn market can be categorized into different types based on the processing method. Carded yarn is made by aligning and straightening cotton fibres with a carding machine, resulting in a fluffy and less uniform yarn. Combed yarn, on the other hand, involves an additional step of combing the fibres to remove short fibres and impurities, resulting in a smoother and stronger yarn. Other types of cotton yarn in the market include ring-spun yarn, open-end yarn, and compact yarn, each with its characteristics and uses.</p></p>
