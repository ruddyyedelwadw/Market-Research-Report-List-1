<p><p>Electro-Mechanical Actuators consist of two main types, Rotary Actuators and Linear Actuators. Rotary Actuators convert electrical energy into rotational motion, while Linear Actuators convert electrical energy into linear motion. Rotary Actuators are commonly used in applications requiring rotational movement, such as robotics and manufacturing equipment. On the other hand, Linear Actuators are used in applications that require linear movement, such as in industrial machinery and automotive systems. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in various industries by providing precise and controlled motion.</p></p>
