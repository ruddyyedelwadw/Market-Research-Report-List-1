<p><p>Fully Automatic Control toilet flushes are equipped with sensors that detect when the toilet is used and automatically flush the water once the user is finished. This type of market is convenient and hygienic, eliminating the need for manual flushing. On the other hand, Water-Saving Hybrid Control toilet flushes allow users to choose between a full flush for solid waste and a partial flush for liquid waste, helping to conserve water. Both markets cater to different consumer preferences for convenience and sustainability in toilet functionality.</p></p>
