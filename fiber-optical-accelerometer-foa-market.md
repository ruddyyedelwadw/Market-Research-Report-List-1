<p><strong>The Fiber Optical Accelerometer (FOA) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-Axis</li><li>Single-Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optical Accelerometer (FOA) market consists of two main types: Multi-Axis and Single-Axis accelerometers. Multi-Axis accelerometers can measure acceleration in multiple directions simultaneously, offering a comprehensive view of movement in various dimensions. On the other hand, Single-Axis accelerometers can only measure acceleration in one specific direction. Multi-Axis accelerometers are commonly used in applications where movement is complex and multidimensional, while Single-Axis accelerometers are suitable for simpler applications that only require measurement in one specific direction.</p></p>
