<p><p>Car head restraints come in two main types: fixed and adjustable. Fixed car head restraints are built into the seat and cannot be adjusted, providing consistent protection for the occupant's head. On the other hand, adjustable car head restraints can be moved up or down to accommodate different heights and preferences, offering a customized level of comfort and safety. Both types play a crucial role in minimizing the risk of whiplash and other neck injuries in the event of a rear-end collision.</p></p>
