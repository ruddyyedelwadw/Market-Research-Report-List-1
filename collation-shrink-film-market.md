<p><strong>The Collation Shrink Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Collation Shrink Film</li><li>Multilayer Collation Shrink Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Collation shrink films are commonly classified into two types: Monolayer and Multilayer. Monolayer collation shrink film is made of a single layer of material, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, Multilayer collation shrink film consists of multiple layers, providing added strength, barrier properties, and versatility for various packaging applications. Both types of films are used for bundling and palletizing products to ensure secure transportation and presentation. The choice between monolayer and multilayer collation shrink films depends on specific packaging requirements and budget considerations.</p></p>
