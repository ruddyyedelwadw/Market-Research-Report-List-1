<p><p>Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCR) are electronic devices used in power control and conversion systems. There are two main types of SCR markets: unidirectional SCR and bidirectional SCR. Unidirectional SCR is a device that allows current flow only in one direction, often used in rectifier circuits. On the other hand, bidirectional SCR permits current flow in both directions and is commonly utilized in AC voltage regulation applications. These two market types cater to different needs and play crucial roles in modern power electronics.</p></p>
