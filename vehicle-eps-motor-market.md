<p><strong>The Vehicle EPS Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CEPS Motor</li><li>REPS Motor</li><li>PEPS Motor</li><li>EHPS Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle EPS motors refer to different types of electric power steering motors used in vehicles for easy steering control. CEPS motors are column-type motors, REPS motors are rack-type motors, PEPS motors are pinion-type motors, and EHPS motors are electro-hydraulic power steering motors. Each type offers unique advantages and disadvantages based on the vehicle's size, weight, and power requirements. Choosing the right type of EPS motor is essential for achieving optimal steering performance and efficiency.</p></p>
