<p><p>Friction Stir Welding (FSW) machine is a specialized welding technique used to join two or more metals without using a filler material. The process involves a non-consumable rotating tool that generates frictional heat and plasticizes the material, leading to a strong and defect-free weld. FSW machines are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, shipbuilding, and construction.</p><p>The market for Friction Stir Welding (FSW) machines is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for lightweight and high-strength materials in the aerospace and automotive industries is a major driving factor for market growth. Friction Stir Welding offers several advantages over traditional welding methods, such as lower distortion, improved mechanical properties, and reduced energy consumption, which further boosts its adoption.</p><p>Additionally, stringent regulations regarding emissions and fuel efficiency in the automotive sector are also propelling the market growth. Manufacturers are increasingly adopting FSW machines to produce lightweight structures that enhance fuel economy.
