<p><p>The IoT and Connected Car market includes various types of connectivity such as Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V), Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I), Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P), and Vehicle-to-Network (V2N). V2V enables communication between vehicles to improve safety and traffic efficiency. V2I facilitates communication between vehicles and infrastructure like traffic signals and road signs. V2P involves interactions between vehicles and pedestrians to enhance pedestrian safety. V2N refers to the connection between vehicles and network services for features like real-time traffic updates and remote diagnostics.</p></p>
