<p><p>Solid-state AC Surge Protective Devices are designed using only solid-state components for protection against transient overvoltages. These devices are known for their fast response times and high energy handling capabilities. On the other hand, Hybrid AC Surge Protective Devices combine both solid-state and gas discharge technologies to provide enhanced protection. They are capable of handling high surge currents while offering superior protection against voltage spikes. Both types of devices are widely used in various applications to safeguard electrical equipment from damage caused by power surges.</p></p>
