<p><p>Non-woven fabrics market can be categorized into various types such as Spunbond Non-Woven Fabric, Spunlace Non-Woven Fabric, Needle Punch Non-Woven Fabric, Meltblown Non-Woven Fabric, Wet Laid Non-Woven Fabric, and Others. Spunbond fabric is made by bonding fibers together through heat and pressure, while spunlace fabric is created through a hydroentanglement process. Needle punch fabric is produced by mechanically interlocking fibers, and meltblown fabric is made by blowing molten polymer through a nozzle. Wet laid fabric involves fibers being dispersed in water and then bonded together.</p></p>
