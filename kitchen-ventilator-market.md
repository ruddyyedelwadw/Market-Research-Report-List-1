<p><strong>The Kitchen Ventilator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Side Suction</li><li>Near Suction</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kitchen ventilators can be categorized into three main types: Side Suction, Near Suction, and Others. Side Suction ventilators are mounted on the wall and draw air through vents located on the sides of the unit. Near Suction ventilators are placed directly above the cooking area and pull air and fumes through a filter system. The Others category includes unique designs or hybrid models that combine features of both Side and Near Suction systems. Each type of ventilator offers different benefits and is suitable for different kitchen layouts.</p></p>
