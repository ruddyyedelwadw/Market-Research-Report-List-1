<p><p>Riveted gratings are commonly available in two main types: smooth surface and blade surface. Smooth surface gratings feature a flat top surface, providing a stable platform for various applications. On the other hand, blade surface gratings have serrated edges that offer better traction and slip resistance, making them ideal for areas with heavy foot traffic or where there is a risk of slipping. Both types of gratings are durable, versatile, and commonly used in industrial, commercial, and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
