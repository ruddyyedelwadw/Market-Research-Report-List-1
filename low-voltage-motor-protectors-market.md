<p><p>Low Voltage Motor Protectors are devices used to protect motors from damage due to overload, overheating, and other electrical faults. Overload relays are used to monitor the current drawn by the motor and trip if the current exceeds a certain threshold. Temperature relays monitor the temperature of the motor and trip if it exceeds a safe limit. Electronic motor protectors combine features of both overload and temperature relays, providing comprehensive protection for motors against various potential issues. These protectors are crucial in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of motors.</p></p>
