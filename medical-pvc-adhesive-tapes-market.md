<p><p>Medical PVC adhesive tapes are categorized into two main types - single sided and double sided. Single sided tapes have adhesive on only one side, making them suitable for securing dressings and medical devices in place. Double sided tapes have adhesive on both sides, allowing for applications such as attaching bandages to skin or joining two surfaces together. Both types of tapes are commonly used in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings for wound care and medical procedures.</p></p>
