<p><p>The MPI Flexible Printed Circuit Market consists of three main types: Single-sided Circuit, Double-sided Circuit, and Multi-layer Circuit. Single-sided Circuits have conductive material on only one side of the substrate, making them the simplest and most cost-effective option. Double-sided Circuits have conductive material on both sides, allowing for more complex designs and higher density of components. Multi-layer Circuits have multiple layers of conductive material separated by insulation layers, offering even greater design flexibility and capability for more advanced electronic devices.</p></p>
