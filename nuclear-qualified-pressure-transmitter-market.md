<p><p>Nuclear qualified pressure transmitters are designed to withstand the harsh conditions found in nuclear power plants. These transmitters come in three main types: single-channel, dual-channel, and tri-channel. Single-channel transmitters provide basic pressure monitoring capabilities, while dual-channel transmitters offer redundancy for added safety and reliability. Tri-channel transmitters provide the highest level of redundancy and can monitor pressure from multiple sources simultaneously. Each type of transmitter is crucial in ensuring accurate and reliable pressure measurement in nuclear power plant applications.</p></p>
