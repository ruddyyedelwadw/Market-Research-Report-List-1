<p><p>A thermos flask, also known as a vacuum flask or insulated bottle, is a container that is used to keep liquids hot or cold for an extended period of time. It is typically made of a double-walled glass or stainless steel container with a vacuum between the walls to prevent heat transfer. Thermos flasks are commonly used for carrying hot beverages such as coffee, tea, or soup, as well as cold beverages like water or juice.</p><p>The Thermos Flask Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.2% during the forecast period due to the increasing demand for convenient and portable containers that can keep beverages at desired temperatures for longer durations. The market growth is also driven by factors such as technological advancements in insulation materials, rising consumer awareness about sustainable and eco-friendly products, and the growing trend of outdoor activities and travel.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Thermos Flask Market is the introduction of smart thermos flasks that come equipped with features such as temperature control, Bluetooth connectivity, and integration with smartphone apps. These smart flasks allow users to monitor and control the temperature of their beverages remotely, adding a new level of convenience and customization to the traditional thermos flask. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on creating stylish and aesthetically pleasing designs to appeal to a wider range of consumers.</p></p>
