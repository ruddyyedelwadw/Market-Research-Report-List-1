<p><strong>The Variable Attenuators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diode Based Attenuators</li><li>MMIC Based Attenuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable attenuators are devices used in electronic circuits to control the amplitude of a signal passing through them. Diode-based attenuators utilize diodes to adjust the signal's amplitude, providing a variable level of attenuation. On the other hand, MMIC-based attenuators use monolithic microwave integrated circuits to achieve the same function. Both types of attenuators offer precise control over signal amplitudes in various applications, such as telecommunications, test and measurement, and radar systems.</p></p>
