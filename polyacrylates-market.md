<p><ul><li>Poly(Ethyl Acrylate)</li><li>Poly(Methyl Acrylate)</li><li>Poly(Butyl Acrylate)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyacrylates refer to a group of polymers with repeating acrylate units. Three main types are Poly(Ethyl Acrylate), Poly(Methyl Acrylate), and Poly(Butyl Acrylate). Poly(Ethyl Acrylate) is known for its flexibility and impact resistance, making it suitable for adhesives and sealants. Poly(Methyl Acrylate) is commonly used in paints, coatings, and adhesives due to its clarity and weather resistance. Poly(Butyl Acrylate) is valued for its flexibility and water resistance, making it ideal for applications like textiles and adhesives.</p></p>
