<p><p>Electric stacker trucks are popular in two main markets - the counterbalance market and the warehouse market. Counterbalance stacker trucks are designed with a weight at the back of the truck to balance the weight of the load being carried, allowing for easy maneuverability in tight spaces. On the other hand, warehouse stacker trucks are specifically designed for use in warehouses, with features such as narrow aisles and high lift capacities to maximize storage space efficiency. Both types of electric stacker trucks offer efficient and reliable handling of materials in their respective markets.</p></p>
