<p><p>Hot-filled stand-up pouches come in different types depending on the shape and bottom design. Round bottom pouches are cylindrical in shape and have a rounded bottom for stability. Roll stock pouches are preformed and sealed at the top, requiring filling and sealing at the bottom. K-style pouches have a flat bottom with a diagonal seal for added strength. Plow/folded bottom pouches have a folded bottom for added durability. Flat bottom pouches have a square bottom for easy display. Other types include special designs tailored to specific product requirements.</p></p>
