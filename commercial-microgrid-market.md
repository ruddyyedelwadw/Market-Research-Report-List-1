<p><p>Commercial microgrids can be classified into three main types: AC microgrid system, DC microgrid system, and hybrid microgrid system. AC microgrid systems operate using alternating current, while DC microgrid systems utilize direct current. Hybrid microgrid systems combine both AC and DC components to provide a more flexible and reliable energy solution. Each type of microgrid system has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on factors such as energy efficiency, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
