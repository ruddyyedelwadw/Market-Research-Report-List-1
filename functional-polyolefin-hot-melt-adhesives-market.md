<p><ul><li>HMA Granular</li><li>HMA Powder</li><li>HMA Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Functional polyolefin hot melt adhesives are available in three main types - HMA granular, HMA powder, and HMA film. HMA granular is in solid form and is melted before application, providing good bond strength. HMA powder is finely ground and activated by heat, offering excellent adhesive properties. HMA film is pre-formed and can be easily applied in precise amounts. Each type has its own unique characteristics making them suitable for different applications in industries such as packaging, construction, and automotive.</p></p>
