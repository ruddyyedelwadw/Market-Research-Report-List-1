<p><p>Sodium chloride market includes three main types: Solar salt, which is produced through evaporation of seawater or brine from salt lakes; Rock salt, which is mined from underground salt deposits; and Vacuum salt, which is produced through the evaporation of brine in a vacuum chamber. Each type of salt has its own unique characteristics and applications. Solar salt is typically used in food processing and water softening, while rock salt is commonly used for de-icing roads and sidewalks. Vacuum salt is often used in the food industry for seasoning and preservation.</p></p>
