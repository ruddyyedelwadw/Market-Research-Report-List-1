The market for FSR is divided into two main types - High Temperature Vulcanization Type and Room Temperature Vulcanization Type. The High Temperature Vulcanization Type requires high heat to cure and offers superior heat resistance, making it suitable for applications that require exposure to high temperatures. On the other hand, the Room Temperature Vulcanization Type cures at lower temperatures and is more convenient for applications that do not require high heat resistance.</p></p>
