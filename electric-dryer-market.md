<p><strong>The Electric Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat pump dryers</li><li>Condenser dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric dryer market types include heat pump dryers and condenser dryers. Heat pump dryers use a heat exchange system to recycle hot air, making them more energy efficient and environmentally friendly. They are also gentler on clothes, as they dry at lower temperatures. Condenser dryers, on the other hand, do not require external ventilation, as they condense moisture from the clothes into a removable container. They are a great option for homes without access to ventilation systems. Both types offer efficient and convenient drying solutions for households.</p></p>
