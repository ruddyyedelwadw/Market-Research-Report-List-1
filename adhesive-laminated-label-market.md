<p><p>Adhesive laminated labels are available in two main types: reels and sheets. Labels in reels are wound into a roll and are commonly used in automated labeling processes for high volume production. Labels in sheets are individual labels arranged on a flat sheet, suitable for manual application or smaller scale labeling needs. Both types offer flexibility in size, shape, and design, making them versatile for various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and logistics.</p></p>
