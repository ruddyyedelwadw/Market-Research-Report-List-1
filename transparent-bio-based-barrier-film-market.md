<p><p>The Transparent Bio-Based Barrier Film market includes two main types: Monolayer and Multilayer films. Monolayer films are made of a single layer of bio-based material, providing basic barrier properties. Multilayer films, on the other hand, are composed of multiple layers of bio-based materials, offering enhanced barrier properties against oxygen, moisture, and other contaminants. Multilayer films are often preferred in applications where higher levels of protection and extended shelf-life are required, while Monolayer films are suitable for more straightforward packaging needs.</p></p>
