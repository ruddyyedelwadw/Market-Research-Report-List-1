<p><p>LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) mapping utilizes laser technology to accurately measure distances and create detailed 3D maps of various terrains and environments. In the mapping market, there are two main types of LiDAR systems: Solid State LiDAR and Mechanical LiDAR. </p><p>Solid State LiDAR systems use semiconductor technology to emit and receive laser pulses, resulting in faster data collection and more compact designs. On the other hand, Mechanical LiDAR systems utilize moving parts to steer laser beams, offering higher accuracy but at a slower data collection rate.</p></p>
