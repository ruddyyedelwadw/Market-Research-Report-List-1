<p><strong>The Integrated Circuit Optical Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nonlinear Photoelectric Coupler</li><li>Linear Photoelectric Coupler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Circuit Optical Couplers are electronic components used to transfer digital or analog signals between different circuits while ensuring electrical isolation. Nonlinear Photoelectric Couplers use semiconductor devices to convert signals into light pulses, offering high-speed data transfer. Linear Photoelectric Couplers use photodiodes to convert signals into proportional current, allowing for accurate signal transmission. Both types are crucial for applications requiring isolation between circuits, such as in power electronics, telecommunications, and industrial automation.</p></p>
