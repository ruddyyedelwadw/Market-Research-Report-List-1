<p><strong>The Passenger Tire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Tire</li><li>Bias Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger tire market consists of two main types - Radial and Bias tires. Radial tires have fabric layers laid at 90 degrees to the tread direction, providing better stability and longer tread life. Bias tires have fabric layers crosshatched at an angle, offering a smoother ride but less durability than radial tires. Radial tires are more common in modern vehicles due to their superior performance and efficiency. Bias tires are still used in certain applications where cost and ride comfort are prioritized over longevity and fuel efficiency.</p></p>
