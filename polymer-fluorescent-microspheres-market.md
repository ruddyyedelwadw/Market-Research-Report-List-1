<p><p>Polymer fluorescent microspheres are categorized into two types - organic and inorganic. Organic microspheres are typically made from synthetic polymers and have a higher level of biocompatibility. They are commonly used in biological and medical applications. On the other hand, inorganic microspheres are composed of materials like silica or glass and are known for their stability and durability. They are more commonly used in industrial applications such as flow cytometry and microscopy. Both types offer unique advantages and are utilized in various fields.</p></p>
