<p><p>Pharmaceutical foil market consists of different types such as Cold Forming Aluminum Foil and Tropical Aluminum Foil. Cold Forming Aluminum Foil is used for packaging extremely sensitive drugs, as it offers high barrier protection against light, moisture, and gases. On the other hand, Tropical Aluminum Foil is designed to withstand high temperatures and humidity, making it suitable for tropical climates. Both types of foil play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and effectiveness of pharmaceutical products, catering to specific packaging needs in the industry.</p></p>
