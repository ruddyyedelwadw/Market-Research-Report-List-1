<p><ul><li>Alkaline Water Electrolysis</li><li>Proton Exchange Membrane</li><li>Solid Oxide Electrolysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Green Ammonia market types include Alkaline Water Electrolysis, Proton Exchange Membrane, and Solid Oxide Electrolysis. Alkaline Water Electrolysis involves splitting water molecules using an alkaline electrolyte solution. Proton Exchange Membrane technology uses a solid polymer electrolyte membrane to conduct protons and separate gases. Solid Oxide Electrolysis operates at high temperatures and uses a solid oxide ceramic electrolyte. These technologies play a key role in the production of green ammonia, which has the potential to reduce carbon emissions in various industries.</p></p>
