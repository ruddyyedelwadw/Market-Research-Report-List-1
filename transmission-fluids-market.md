<p><p>Transmission fluids can be categorized into three types based on their composition: mineral, synthetic, and semi-synthetic. Mineral transmission fluids are derived from refined crude oil and are the most basic form of transmission fluid. Synthetic transmission fluids are chemically engineered to offer superior performance and protection for the transmission system. Semi-synthetic transmission fluids are a blend of mineral and synthetic fluids, offering a balance between performance and cost-effectiveness. Each type of transmission fluid caters to different needs and preferences of consumers in the market.</p></p>
