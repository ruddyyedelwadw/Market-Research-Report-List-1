<p><p>Normal-speed quenching oils are commonly categorized into two main types in the market - Straight Mineral Oil Type and Compounded Type. Straight Mineral Oil Type quenching oils are formulated solely from mineral oil without any additives, offering good cooling properties. On the other hand, Compounded Type quenching oils contain additives such as polymers, surfactants, and anti-oxidants, providing enhanced cooling and quenching performance. Both types cater to various industrial applications where controlled cooling rates are required to achieve desired metallurgical properties in the final product.</p></p>
