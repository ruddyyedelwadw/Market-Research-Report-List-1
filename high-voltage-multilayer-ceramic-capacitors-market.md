<p><p>High Voltage Multilayer Ceramic Capacitors market can be classified into two types: chip type and lead type. Chip type capacitors are compact in size and suitable for high-density applications where space is limited. They are ideal for surface mount technology. Lead type capacitors have wire leads for easy connections and can handle higher voltages. Both types offer high reliability, temperature stability, and low equivalent series resistance, making them ideal for a wide range of high voltage applications in various industries.</p></p>
