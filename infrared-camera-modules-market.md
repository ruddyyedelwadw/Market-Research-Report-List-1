<p><p>Infrared camera modules can be categorized into three types based on the wavelength they detect: shortwave, medium wave, and long wave. Shortwave infrared cameras are used for military applications and night vision, as they can detect shorter wavelengths of light. Medium wave cameras are commonly used in industrial and scientific applications due to their ability to capture a wider range of temperatures. Long wave infrared cameras are used for thermal imaging in various industries, as they are sensitive to longer wavelengths of light.</p></p>
