<p><strong>The Military Aircraft Weighing Scales Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platform</li><li>Floor-standing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platform weighing scales are military aircraft weighing scales that are designed to be mounted on the platform or ground. These scales are commonly used for weighing smaller military aircraft and are designed to be easily transportable. They typically have a flat platform surface where the aircraft can be positioned for weighing.</p><p>Floor-standing weighing scales, on the other hand, are designed to be fixed in a specific location. These scales are used for weighing larger military aircraft and are usually installed in hangers or other dedicated facilities. They are more robust and capable of handling heavier aircraft, providing accurate and reliable weight measurements.</p></p>
