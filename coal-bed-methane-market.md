<p><p>Coal Bed Methane (CBM) is extracted from coal beds using various methods. Horizontal drilling involves drilling wells horizontally to increase the production of CBM. Hydraulic fracturing is a process that involves injecting water, sand, and chemicals into the coal seams to release methane. The Proppants market provides materials like sand or ceramic beads which are used in hydraulic fracturing to keep the fractures open and allow the gas to flow out. These methods are crucial in extracting CBM efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
