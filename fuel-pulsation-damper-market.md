<p><strong>The Fuel Pulsation Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Tube Type</li><li>Twin-Tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel pulsation dampers are a crucial component in a vehicle's fuel system to regulate fuel pressure and reduce vibrations. There are two main types in the market - single-tube type and twin-tube type. Single-tube type dampers have a single chamber for dampening fuel pressure fluctuations, while twin-tube type dampers have two chambers to provide more effective dampening. Both types are designed to maintain stable fuel pressure and improve engine performance by reducing pressure variations caused by fuel pump operation.</p></p>
