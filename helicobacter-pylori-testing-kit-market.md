The antibody test detects the presence of antibodies produced by the body in response to an H. pylori infection, while the antigen test detects specific proteins from the bacteria itself. Both tests are commonly used in diagnosing H. pylori infection and are quick and convenient options for healthcare providers to determine the presence of the bacteria in patients.</p></p>
