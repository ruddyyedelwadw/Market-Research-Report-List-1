<p><p>Trivex lenses are available in different market types based on the user's vision needs. Single vision lenses are designed for correcting either nearsightedness or farsightedness, providing clear vision at a single distance. Bifocal lenses, on the other hand, have two different prescriptions in one lens to correct both distance and close-up vision. These lenses cater to individuals with presbyopia who require assistance for both near and distance vision. Both market types offer excellent clarity and durability for everyday use.</p></p>
