<p><p>Automotive tubeless tires come in two main types: radial tubeless tires and bias tubeless tires. Radial tubeless tires have cords that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, allowing for better traction, handling, and fuel efficiency. Bias tubeless tires, on the other hand, have cords that run diagonal to the direction of travel, providing a more comfortable ride. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific needs of the vehicle and driving conditions.</p></p>
