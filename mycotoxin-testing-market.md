<p><ul><li>HPLC-Based Technology</li><li>LC-MS/MS-Based Technology</li><li>Immunoassay-Based Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mycotoxin Testing Market includes three main types of technologies: HPLC-Based Technology, LC-MS/MS-Based Technology, and Immunoassay-Based Technology. HPLC-Based Technology uses high-performance liquid chromatography to separate and quantify mycotoxins. LC-MS/MS-Based Technology combines liquid chromatography with tandem mass spectrometry to detect multiple mycotoxins with high sensitivity and specificity. Immunoassay-Based Technology utilizes antibodies to detect and quantify mycotoxins. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of food and feed products by detecting harmful mycotoxins.</p></p>
