<p><p>Hermetic pressure sensors are classified into three main types based on the pressure range they are designed to measure: low pressure, mid pressure, and high pressure sensors. Low pressure sensors are typically used to measure pressures below 15 psi, while mid pressure sensors are used for pressures between 15 psi and 1000 psi. High-pressure sensors, on the other hand, are designed to measure pressures above 1000 psi. Each type of sensor is specially engineered to provide accurate and reliable pressure measurements within its designated range.</p></p>
