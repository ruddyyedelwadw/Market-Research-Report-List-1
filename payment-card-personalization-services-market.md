<p><p>Payment card personalization services market offers various types of printing technologies such as Dye Sub, Retransfer, and Inkjet. Dye Sub technology uses heat to transfer dye onto cards, producing high-quality images. Retransfer technology prints on a film that is then transferred onto the card, providing better durability. Inkjet technology directly prints onto the card surface, offering quick and cost-effective customization. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, ensuring a wide range of options for card personalization.</p></p>
