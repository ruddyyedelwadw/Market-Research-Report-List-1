<p><p>Wearable medical device adhesives are primarily categorized into four types: Silicon-Based, Acrylic Based, Hydrocolloid, and Synthetic Based Rubber. Silicon-Based adhesives are known for their gentle adhesion and flexibility. Acrylic Based adhesives offer strong adhesion and are often used for long-term wear. Hydrocolloid adhesives are suitable for sensitive skin as they are breathable and hypoallergenic. Synthetic Based Rubber adhesives provide excellent adhesion to a variety of surfaces. Each type of adhesive offers unique properties to cater to different needs in the wearable medical device market.</p></p>
