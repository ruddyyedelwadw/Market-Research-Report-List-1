<p><p>Smart automotive headlights come in two main types: Adaptive Front Lighting Headlights and Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights. </p><p>Adaptive Front Lighting Headlights adjust the direction of the light beam based on the vehicle's speed, steering angle, and road conditions to improve visibility and safety while driving at night or in low visibility conditions. </p><p>Adaptive Driving Beam Headlights automatically switch between high and low beams to prevent blinding oncoming drivers, while still providing optimal visibility for the driver. Both types of headlights enhance overall driving experience and safety.</p></p>
