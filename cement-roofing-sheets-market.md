<p><p>Cement roofing sheets come in three main types: corrugated, plain, and tiles. Corrugated sheets have ridges for added strength and water runoff, making them popular for industrial and agricultural buildings. Plain sheets have a smooth surface and are commonly used in residential roofing. Tile sheets mimic the appearance of traditional clay tiles but are more affordable and durable. Each type of sheet caters to different market segments based on their specific properties and aesthetic preferences.</p></p>
