<p><p>TFT LCD Display Modules are available in various types, including A-MVA, ASV, MVA, S-PVA, and P-IPS. A-MVA (Advanced Multi-domain Vertical Alignment) offers high image quality with wide viewing angles. ASV (Advanced Super View) provides enhanced image clarity and color reproduction. MVA (Multi-domain Vertical Alignment) displays offer improved response times and contrast ratios. S-PVA (Super Patterned Vertical Alignment) screens deliver deeper blacks and better color accuracy. P-IPS (Professional In-Plane Switching) modules are known for their superior color reproduction and wide viewing angles. Each type caters to different needs in the market.</p></p>
