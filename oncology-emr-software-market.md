<p><p>Oncology EMR software market can be categorized into two types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operated on the user's own servers or devices. It requires maintenance, upgrades, and backups to be managed by the user's IT team. In contrast, Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet. It offers the advantages of easy scalability, automatic updates, and accessible data from any location. While On-premise offers more control, Cloud-based solutions provide flexibility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
