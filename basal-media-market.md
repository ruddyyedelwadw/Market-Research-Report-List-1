<p><p>Basal media refer to the basic culture medium used for growing cells in a laboratory setting. The different types of basal media include classical media & salts, serum-free media, stem cell media, and other specialized media. Classical media & salts contain essential nutrients and salts for cell growth. Serum-free media do not contain any animal-derived components, making them suitable for specific cell culture applications. Stem cell media are designed to support the growth and differentiation of stem cells. Other specialized media cater to the specific requirements of different cell types or research purposes.</p></p>
