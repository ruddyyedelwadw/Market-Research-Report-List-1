<p><p>The Automotive CVT Parts Market can be categorized into various types including Valve Body, Stepper Motor, Pumps, Planet Sets, Drive Belt, Push Belt, and Others. The Valve Body controls the flow of hydraulic fluid in the transmission. Stepper Motors aid in the adjustment of transmission ratios. Pumps ensure proper fluid circulation. Planet Sets help in providing different gear ratios. Drive Belts transfer power between pulleys. Push Belts assist in power transmission. Other parts include bearings, seals, and sensors that contribute to the overall functioning of the CVT system.</p></p>
