<p><p>AC-DC converters for aerospace can be classified into two main types: single output and multiple output converters. Single output converters are designed to provide a single output voltage level, making them suitable for applications that require a consistent power supply. On the other hand, multiple output converters are capable of providing two or more output voltage levels simultaneously, offering flexibility and efficiency in powering different components or systems within an aerospace vehicle or device. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring reliable and stable power distribution in aerospace applications.</p></p>
