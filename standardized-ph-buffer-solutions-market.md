<p><p>Standardized pH buffer solutions are available in two main types: acidic buffer solutions and alkaline buffer solutions. Acidic buffer solutions are used to calibrate pH meters and sensors in the acidic pH range, typically below pH 7. Alkaline buffer solutions, on the other hand, are used for calibration in the alkaline pH range, typically above pH 7. Both types of buffer solutions play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable pH measurements across a variety of industries, including research, healthcare, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
