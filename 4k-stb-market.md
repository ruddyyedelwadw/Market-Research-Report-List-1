<p><p>IPTV, Satellite, DTT, OTT, and Hybrid are the five main types of 4K STB markets. IPTV utilizes internet technology to deliver television content. Satellite STBs receive signals via satellite dish. DTT STBs capture signals through aerial antenna. OTT STBs stream content directly over the internet. Hybrid STBs combine multiple technologies such as IPTV, satellite, and DTT to offer a wider range of content options. Each market type offers unique features and benefits to cater to different consumer preferences.</p></p>
