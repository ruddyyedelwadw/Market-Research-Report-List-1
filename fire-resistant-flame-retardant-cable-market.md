<p><p>Fire resistant flame retardant cables are categorized into two main types: halogen-containing cables and halogen-free cables. Halogen-containing cables are made with materials such as PVC, which contain halogen elements like chlorine and bromine. These cables release harmful gases when burned. On the other hand, halogen-free cables are made with materials that do not contain halogen elements, making them environmentally friendly and safe in case of a fire. They are gaining popularity due to their superior fire safety properties compared to halogen-containing cables.</p></p>
