<p><p>Automotive TCU market types include AT (Automatic Transmission), CVT (Continuous Variable Transmission), and DCT (Dual Clutch Transmission). AT is the most common type, providing smooth gear changes without manual input. CVT offers seamless acceleration due to its ability to continuously vary gear ratios. DCT combines the efficiency of manual transmission with the convenience of automatic transmission, providing quick and smooth gear shifts. Each market type caters to different preferences and driving styles, offering varying levels of performance and efficiency.</p></p>
