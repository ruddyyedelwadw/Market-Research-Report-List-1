<p><p>Directional sign markets consist of two main types: wall-mounted and floor-standing signs. Wall-mounted signs are typically fixed to walls or other vertical surfaces and are ideal for providing guidance in narrow or crowded spaces. They are commonly used in indoor settings like shopping malls and office buildings. On the other hand, floor-standing signs are free-standing displays that can be placed on the ground and are often used in larger open spaces like airports or outdoor events to direct foot traffic. Both types play a crucial role in guiding people to their desired destinations.</p></p>
