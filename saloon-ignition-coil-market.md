<p><strong>The Saloon Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Magnetic</li><li>Closed Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Saloon ignition coils come in two main types - open magnetic and closed magnetic. Open magnetic ignition coils have a visible core and are more commonly found in older vehicles, providing a simpler design and easier maintenance. Closed magnetic ignition coils have a sealed core and are more commonly used in modern vehicles, offering better protection against external elements and increased durability. Both types serve the same purpose of converting low voltage into high voltage to ignite the fuel mixture in the engine.</p></p>
