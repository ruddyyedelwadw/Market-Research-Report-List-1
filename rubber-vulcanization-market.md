<p><ul><li>Accelerator</li><li>Vulcanizing Agent</li><li>Activator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber vulcanization market is segmented into Accelerator, Vulcanizing Agent, and Activator types. Accelerators are used to hasten the vulcanization process, improving the efficiency and reducing curing time. Vulcanizing agents react with the rubber to form crosslinks, enhancing its strength and elasticity. Activators assist in the activation of vulcanizing agents to facilitate the crosslinking process. Each type plays a crucial role in the rubber vulcanization process, contributing to the overall quality and performance of the final rubber product.</p></p>
