<p><p>Single-to-double strollers come in three main types: side-by-sides, in-lines, and convertibles. Side-by-side strollers have seats next to each other, allowing both children to have equal views. In-line strollers have one seat behind the other, making them easier to maneuver through narrow spaces. Convertible strollers can be used as a single or double stroller by adding an extra seat when needed. Each type offers unique features to accommodate the needs of parents with multiple children.</p></p>
