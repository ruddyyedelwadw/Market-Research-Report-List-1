<p><p>The tire gauge market can be categorized into two main types: digital and analog. Digital tire gauges use electronic sensors to provide precise and accurate readings of tire pressure. They often come with advanced features such as backlit displays and automatic shut-off. On the other hand, analog tire gauges use mechanical mechanisms to measure tire pressure and typically have a traditional dial or gauge face. Both types of tire gauges are popular among consumers, with digital gauges offering convenience and accuracy, while analog gauges are known for their simplicity and durability.</p></p>
