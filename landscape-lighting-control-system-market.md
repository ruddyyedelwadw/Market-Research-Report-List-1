<p><p>The landscape lighting control system market is categorized into two types: wireless control and wired control. Wireless control refers to a system where the lighting fixtures are operated and controlled through wireless communication technologies such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, or radio frequency. This type of control offers flexibility, ease of installation, and scalability. On the other hand, wired control consists of a system where the lighting fixtures are connected through physical cables, allowing for direct control and reliable performance. Wired control may require more complex installation but ensures stable and uninterrupted communication.</p></p>
