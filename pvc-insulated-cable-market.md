<p><p>PVC insulated cables are widely used in the market, with two main types being PVC sheathed armoured cable and PVC sheathed unarmoured cable. PVC sheathed armoured cables are typically used in applications where greater protection against physical damage is required, such as underground installations or outdoor environments. On the other hand, PVC sheathed unarmoured cables are used in less demanding environments where protection against mechanical stress is not as critical. Both types of cables offer reliable electrical insulation and are cost-effective solutions for various applications.</p></p>
