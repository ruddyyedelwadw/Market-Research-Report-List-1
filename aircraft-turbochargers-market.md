<p><ul><li>Butterfly Valve Type</li><li>Poppet Valve Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft turbochargers are available in two main types - Butterfly Valve Type and Poppet Valve Type. Butterfly Valve Type turbochargers feature a disc-shaped valve that rotates to control airflow, providing efficient and precise adjustment. Poppet Valve Type turbochargers use a spring-loaded valve to regulate airflow, offering a simpler design and reliable performance. Both types are widely used in aircraft engines to increase power and efficiency by compressing air before it enters the combustion chamber.</p></p>
