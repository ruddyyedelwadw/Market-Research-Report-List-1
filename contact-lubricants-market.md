<p><p>Contact lubricants are commonly available in two main types - Polyphenyl Ether (PPE) Type and Other Types. PPE Type lubricants offer excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. On the other hand, Other types of contact lubricants include silicone-based, mineral oil-based, and synthetic oils. These lubricants offer different properties and are used in various industrial applications based on their specific requirements such as temperature resistance, water resistance, and load-bearing capacity.</p></p>
