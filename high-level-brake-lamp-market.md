<p><p>High level brake lamps are an essential safety feature in vehicles, providing visibility to drivers behind. They are available in two main market types: 12V and 24V. The 12V lamps are designed for smaller vehicles, such as passenger cars, while the 24V lamps are intended for larger vehicles like trucks and buses. Both types function similarly, illuminating when the brakes are applied to alert other drivers of a vehicle's deceleration. The choice between 12V and 24V lamps largely depends on the vehicle's electrical system voltage.</p></p>
