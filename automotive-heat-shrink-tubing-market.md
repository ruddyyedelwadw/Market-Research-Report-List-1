<p><p>Automotive Heat Shrink Tubing comes in various types including PTFE, FEP, PFA, ETFE, PET, PEEK, and PTFE/FEP. PTFE tubing offers excellent chemical resistance, high temperature resistance, and low friction properties. FEP tubing is known for its clarity, flexibility, and resistance to heat and chemicals. PFA tubing provides excellent mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties. ETFE tubing is lightweight, tough, and resistant to UV radiation. PET tubing is a cost-effective option with good thermal stability. PEEK tubing offers high temperature resistance and excellent mechanical properties.</p></p>
