<p><p>KGW laser crystals can be categorized into two main types based on the dopant used: Yb:KGW laser crystals and Nd:KGW laser crystals. Yb:KGW laser crystals are doped with ytterbium and are commonly used in high-power lasers for industrial and medical applications. Nd:KGW laser crystals, doped with neodymium, are often used in solid-state lasers for various applications including material processing and scientific research. Both types of KGW laser crystals offer high efficiency and excellent thermal properties, making them versatile options for laser technology.</p></p>
