<p><ul><li>Rotary Heat Exchangers</li><li>Plate Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Heat Recovery Ventilation (HRV) Units come in two main types: Rotary Heat Exchangers and Plate Heat Exchangers. Rotary Heat Exchangers use rotating wheels to transfer heat and moisture between incoming and outgoing air streams. Plate Heat Exchangers, on the other hand, use plates to achieve the same heat transfer process. Both types of HRV units are widely used in commercial buildings to improve indoor air quality and reduce energy costs by recovering heat from exhaust air and transferring it to incoming fresh air.</p></p>
