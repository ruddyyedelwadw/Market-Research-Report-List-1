<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Rod ANPR Camera</li><li>Without Hydraulic Rod ANPR Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ANPR cameras can be categorized into two main types: those with a hydraulic rod and those without. Hydraulic rod ANPR cameras are equipped with a movable arm that can be adjusted to capture license plate information at varying heights. On the other hand, ANPR cameras without a hydraulic rod do not have this feature and are typically mounted in a fixed position. Both types of cameras are used for automatic license plate recognition in various settings such as parking lots, toll booths, and law enforcement.</p></p>
