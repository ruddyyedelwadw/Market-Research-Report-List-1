<p><ul><li>Non-Woven</li><li>Woven</li><li>Knitted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geotextiles market consists of three types: non-woven, woven, and knitted. Non-woven geotextiles are made through a mechanical or chemical bonding process and are used for filtration and separation purposes in construction and civil engineering projects. Woven geotextiles are created by interlacing yarns and offer durability and strength for erosion control and soil stabilization applications. Knitted geotextiles are manufactured using a knitting process, providing flexibility and drainage capabilities for drainage systems and erosion protection. These different types cater to varying needs within the geotextiles market.</p></p>
