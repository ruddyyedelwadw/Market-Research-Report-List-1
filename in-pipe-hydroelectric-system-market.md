<p><ul><li>Internal System</li><li>External System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Pipe Hydroelectric System refers to a technology that generates electricity using water flowing in existing pipelines. The market for these systems can be categorized into two types: internal system and external system. Internal systems are installed within the pipeline, utilizing water pressure to drive turbines and generate energy. External systems, on the other hand, are installed next to the pipeline, with water being diverted to turbines via an inlet pipe. Both types have their own advantages and are suitable for different scenarios, depending on the existing infrastructure and requirements.</p></p>
