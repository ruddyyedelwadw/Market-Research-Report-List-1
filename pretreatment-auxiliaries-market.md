<p><strong>The Pretreatment Auxiliaries Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Activator Agent</li><li>Desizing Agent</li><li>Dispersant Agent</li><li>Cleaner Agent</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pretreatment auxiliaries are used in various processes to prepare the fabric for dyeing or printing. </p><p>Activator agents help in enhancing the dyeing process by improving the uptake of dyes on the fabric. Desizing agents are used to remove sizing agents from the fabric to improve dye penetration. Dispersant agents help in dispersing the dye evenly on the fabric surface. </p><p>Cleaner agents are used to remove impurities and prepare the fabric for further processing. Other types of pretreatment auxiliaries include leveling agents, sequestrants, and wetting agents.</p></p>
