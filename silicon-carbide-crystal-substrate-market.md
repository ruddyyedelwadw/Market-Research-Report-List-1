<p><p>Silicon Carbide Crystal Substrate is available in two main types in the market: Conductive and Semi-insulated. </p><p>Conductive Silicon Carbide Crystal Substrates have high electrical conductivity due to the presence of impurities or intentional doping. These substrates are primarily used in electronic devices where high electrical conductivity is required.</p><p>Semi-insulated Silicon Carbide Crystal Substrates have low electrical conductivity and are used in applications where minimal electrical interference is desired. These substrates are suitable for high-frequency and power devices where insulating properties are important.</p></p>
