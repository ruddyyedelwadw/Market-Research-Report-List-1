<p><ul><li>E/V</li><li>F/V</li><li>A/V</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bicycle pumps can be categorized into three main types based on the valve they are designed to work with: E/V (Schrader valve), F/V (Presta valve), and A/V (Woods/Dunlop valve). E/V pumps are commonly used for mountain bikes and some hybrid bikes, whereas F/V pumps are preferred for road bikes and high-performance bicycles. A/V pumps are less common but are often used for older bikes or in certain regions. Each type of pump is designed to effectively inflate tires with the corresponding valve type.</p></p>
