<p><ul><li>Adjustable Damping Suspension</li><li>Continuous Adjustable Damping Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Motorcycle semi-active suspension systems come in two main types: adjustable damping suspension and continuous adjustable damping suspension. Adjustable damping suspension allows riders to manually adjust the damping settings based on their preferences and riding conditions. On the other hand, continuous adjustable damping suspension systems adjust the damping settings automatically in real-time based on inputs such as road conditions and rider behavior. Both types of systems aim to provide better comfort, handling, and stability for riders while on the road.</p></p>
