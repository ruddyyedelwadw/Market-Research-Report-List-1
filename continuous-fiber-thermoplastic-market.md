<p><p>Continuous Fiber Thermoplastic market includes two main types of reinforcement: Short Fiber Reinforcement and Long Fiber Reinforcement. Short fiber reinforcement involves fibers that are typically less than 1 mm in length, providing improved stiffness and strength to the thermoplastic material. Long fiber reinforcement, on the other hand, includes fibers that are typically greater than 1 mm in length, offering even higher strength and impact resistance to the thermoplastic. Both types of reinforcement play a crucial role in enhancing the mechanical properties of thermoplastic composites.</p></p>
